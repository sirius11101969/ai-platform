# 06 - Component Library

## Purpose

This document defines the baseline components of the AS6 Living Space interface.

## Core Components

- Living sphere
- Central space sphere
- Relationship stream
- Right thinking column
- Bottom recommendation panel
- Conversational input field
- Calm action button
- Presence indicator
- Thinking timeline

## Living Sphere

A sphere represents a business area or a living state.

It must be soft, readable, calm and connected.

## Relationship Stream

A relationship stream is not a line.

It is a living connection between business meanings.

## Right Thinking Column

The right column shows what the space is thinking, not a technical log.

## Bottom Recommendation Panel

The bottom panel explains why the next step matters.
