# 10 - AS6 Design Constitution

## Master Decision

AS6 Screen 3 Living Space is the brand master screen.

Every current and future interface is a state of this master space.

## Immutable Principles

- One living space
- One visual language
- One calm atmosphere
- One component system
- One emotional experience

## Product Rule

Do not design separate pages.

Design states of the living space.

## Calm Rule

When AS6 becomes smarter, the interface must become clearer and calmer.

## Brand Rule

The product must be recognizable as AS6 without the logo.

## Final Rule

If the user feels that the business became clearer, calmer and stronger, the design is working.
