## Reference Meta-Model Alignment

AS6 Engineering Meta-Architecture is described by the AS6 Reference Meta-Model.

It is not a superstandard.

It does not prescribe normative content for AS6 Architecture Standard, AS6 Engineering Lifecycle or AS6 Governance Standard.

It organizes the normative system through structural properties and permissible relationships.

## Final Axiom

Engineering Meta-Architecture organizes the normative system.

Normative Standards define requirements.

Operational Artifacts document the application of those requirements.

Runtime Evidence demonstrates requirement fulfillment.
