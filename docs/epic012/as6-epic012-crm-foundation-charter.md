# AS6 EPIC012 CRM Foundation Charter

EPIC_ID=AS6_EPIC012_CRM_FOUNDATION
EPIC_TYPE=APPLICATION
EPIC_CHARTER=APPROVED
STATUS=APPROVED

Objective:
Create the CRM application foundation on top of stable AS6 platform baselines.

Business value:
- Start practical application engineering after platform engineering completion.
- Prepare CRM modules for contacts, companies, deals, tasks, notes, analytics and automation.
- Validate Operating System V1, Workspace Experience V1 and Application Foundation V1 through a real application domain.

Scope:
- CRM Contract.
- CRM Registry.
- CRM Entity Model.
- CRM Capability Model.
- CRM Context.
- CRM Runtime.
- CRM Navigation Registration.
- CRM Panel Registration.
- CRM Command Registration.
- CRM Diagnostics.
- CRM Runtime Tracer.
- CRM Health Snapshot.

Out of scope:
- Business CRM implementation.
- Persistent storage changes.
- Payment logic.
- AI business decisions.
- Platform baseline mutation.

Mandatory constraints:
PLATFORM_MUTATION=FALSE
PUBLIC_API_CHANGE=FALSE
BASELINE_IMPACT=NONE
