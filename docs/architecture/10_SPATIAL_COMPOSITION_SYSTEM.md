# AS6 Spatial Composition System v1.0

## Purpose

Spatial Composition System defines the physical laws of AS6 Living Space.

It describes how the environment is structured, not how isolated screens are arranged.

## Main Rule

The interface is not placed on top of space.

The interface is born from the space.

## Permanent Layers

### 1. Atmosphere

The deepest layer: light, gradients, depth, volume and background calm.

### 2. Central Geometry

The main semantic object of the current state.

Only one primary center may exist at a time.

### 3. Information Panels

Panels explain the state but never dominate it.

### 4. Tools

Actions, fields, menus and contextual controls appear only when needed.

### 5. Micro Feedback

Small confirmations, hints and indicators remain subtle and calm.

## Spatial Laws

### Law of Center

Every state has one primary attention center.

### Law of Free Space

Empty space is a functional interface element.

It reduces cognitive load and increases clarity.

### Law of Depth

AS6 uses depth as a hierarchy mechanism.

Importance is expressed through spatial level, not visual noise.

### Law of Light

Light structures the environment and guides attention.

No aggressive lighting is allowed.

### Law of Motion

Motion must explain what changed, why it changed and where the object moved.

### Law of Continuity

Transitions preserve atmosphere, scale, depth, material language and brand recognition.

Only central geometry and state-specific information change.

### Law of Panels

Panels are surfaces inside Living Space, not separate windows.

They keep unified material, radius, transparency and shadow behavior.

### Law of Information

Information is disclosed gradually:

1. meaning;
2. structure;
3. details.

### Law of Focus

The user works with one primary task at a time.

Secondary elements automatically lower their visual priority.

## State Approval Checklist

- AS6 is recognizable in less than one second.
- A single center of attention exists.
- The space is not overloaded.
- Master Screen atmosphere is preserved.
- Central geometry is unique.
- Motion helps understanding.
- Emotional stability is preserved.
- The state feels like AS6 Living Space, not a separate page.
