# 13 - Visual Identity Through Geometry

## Purpose

AS6 must feel like one product while every major state remains instantly recognizable.

The brand stays consistent.

The central geometry changes.

## Law

Same brand. Different geometry.

The user must understand two things in less than one second:

1. This is AS6.
2. This is a different working state.

## Constant Brand DNA

All states preserve:

- light blue living atmosphere
- calm typography
- soft glass surfaces
- living light
- right thinking column
- bottom explanation block
- conversational input field
- calm motion language
- human wording

## Variable Central Geometry

The central area must change by state.

It may change through:

- shape
- spacing
- light behavior
- relationship type
- density
- gravity
- motion pattern
- symbolic structure

## Geometry Catalog

### Focus Layout

One strong center.

Concentric attention, lens, ray, calm focus.

Purpose: decide what matters now.

### Thinking Layout

Asymmetric constellation.

Knowledge pieces move toward a forming idea.

Purpose: make a thought become a direction.

### Living Ecosystem Layout

Balanced living network.

Business areas coexist and influence each other.

Purpose: show how the business lives now.

### Analytics Layout

Flow-based insight field.

Streams, layers and measured transitions replace spheres when needed.

Purpose: show patterns and conclusions.

### CRM Layout

Relationship field.

People, contacts and interactions become the central geometry.

Purpose: show relationship health.

### Documents Layout

Knowledge sheets and memory layers.

Documents float as connected surfaces, not as ordinary files.

Purpose: show business memory.

### Timeline Layout

Living river of time.

Events flow as light drops and decision points.

Purpose: show sequence, history and momentum.

## Anti-Confusion Rule

No two major states may use the same central composition.

If a user can confuse two states at a glance, the geometry is not approved.

## Approval Test

Hide all text and logos.

A reviewer should still recognize:

- that the interface belongs to AS6;
- which major state it represents.

If both are true, visual identity passes.

## Final Principle

AS6 is recognized not only by color or logo.

AS6 is recognized by the living geometry of its space.
