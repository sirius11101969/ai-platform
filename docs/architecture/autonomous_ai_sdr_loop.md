# Autonomous AI SDR Loop

## Overview
Autonomous AI SDR loop continuously evaluates conversation intelligence and recommends next-best actions in **suggestion-only mode**.

## Human approval model
- Suggestion Only
- Human Approval Required
- No Automatic Outreach
- No Automatic CRM Execution

## AI orchestration loop
1. Evaluate lead state (cold → hot, enterprise, stalled, risk).
2. Generate next-best actions.
3. Generate follow-up strategy and timing.
4. Recommend human escalation/handoff.
5. Recommend sales stage transition.
6. Persist recommendations and emit realtime events.

## Escalation strategy
Escalation is recommended for high value, enterprise signals, negative sentiment, complex objections, technical requirements, and high pricing sensitivity.
