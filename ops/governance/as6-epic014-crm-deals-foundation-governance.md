# AS6 Governance — EPIC014 CRM Deals Foundation

Stage: AS6_EPIC014_SLICE02_CRM_DEALS_FOUNDATION

- Deals Foundation must remain declarative.
- Registry, manifest, runtime, resolver, navigation, panels and health snapshot are required.
- Deals must reuse Contacts and Companies foundations declaratively.
- Storage, API calls and business workflows are forbidden.
- Own router, own store and parallel shell are forbidden.
- Future UI slices must reuse this foundation.
