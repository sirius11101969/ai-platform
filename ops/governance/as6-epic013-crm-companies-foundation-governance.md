# AS6 Governance — EPIC013 CRM Companies Foundation

Stage: AS6_EPIC013_SLICE02_CRM_COMPANIES_FOUNDATION

- Companies Foundation must remain declarative.
- Registry, manifest, runtime, resolver, navigation, panels and health snapshot are required.
- Storage, API calls and business workflows are forbidden.
- Heavy dependencies are forbidden.
- Contact linkage remains declarative only.
- Future UI slices must reuse this foundation.
