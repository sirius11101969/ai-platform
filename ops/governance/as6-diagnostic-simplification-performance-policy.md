# AS6 Diagnostic Simplification and Performance Policy

Purpose:

Keep AS6 Diagnostics First strong, but simpler, faster, and easier to operate.

Rules:

- Full diagnostics remain the source of truth.
- Operator visibility must have a fast summary path.
- Autonomy score must never exceed 100 percent.
- Diagnostic debt must be visible as explicit classes.
- Legacy patterns are tracked and migrated gradually.
- Weak linkage warnings are tracked as governance debt.
- New diagnostics must reduce uncertainty, not add noise.
- Runtime artifacts must not be staged.
- Backup artifacts must not live in diagnostic discovery paths.
