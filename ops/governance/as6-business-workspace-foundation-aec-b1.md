# AS6 Business Workspace Foundation AEC B1

Failure classes:
- AS6_BUSINESS_WORKSPACE_FOUNDATION_DRIFT
- AS6_BUSINESS_WORKSPACE_API_GAP
- AS6_BUSINESS_MODULE_HOST_GAP
- AS6_BUSINESS_COMMAND_REGISTRY_GAP
- AS6_BUSINESS_CONTEXT_RUNTIME_GAP

AEC rules:
- Business OS modules must integrate through Business Workspace API.
- Existing App/Shell screens must not be replaced during B1.
- Business Workspace must expose module, command, context, favorites, recent and notification primitives.
