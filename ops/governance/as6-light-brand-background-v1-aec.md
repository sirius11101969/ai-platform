# AS6 Light Brand Background v1 — AEC

1. The branded background may render only for `data-theme="light"` and `data-light-background="brand"`.
2. The dark-theme contract must not contain a branded-background selector.
3. Background artwork must remain below product content and use `pointer-events: none`.
4. Brand colours must stay within the cool white/blue-grey AS6 family; warm cream/yellow values are forbidden.
5. The clean pure-white light background must remain available.
6. Background preference must be isolated by workspace identifier and invalid values must resolve to the safe `brand` default.
7. Literal full-logo watermarks are forbidden behind working content; use abstract rings, circuits and glints.
