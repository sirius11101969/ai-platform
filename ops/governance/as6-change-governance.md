# AS6 Change Governance

Every production-impacting change must be controlled before merge or deployment.

Required change controls:
- AS6_CHANGE_IMPACT_ANALYSIS=PASS
- AS6_CHANGE_RISK_CLASSIFICATION=PASS
- AS6_CHANGE_ROLLBACK_PLAN=PASS
- AS6_CHANGE_VALIDATION_PLAN=PASS
- AS6_CHANGE_APPROVAL_GATE=PASS
- AS6_CHANGE_POST_DIAGNOSTICS=PASS

Blocking root cause classes:
- UNCONTROLLED_CHANGE
- MISSING_CHANGE_ROLLBACK_PLAN
- MISSING_CHANGE_VALIDATION_PLAN
- MISSING_CHANGE_IMPACT_ANALYSIS

AEC signal required by diagnostics:
AEC_CHANGE_GOVERNANCE=PASS
