# AS6 Marketplace Production Services AEC P34

Failure classes:
- AS6_MARKETPLACE_PRODUCTION_SERVICES_DRIFT
- AS6_MARKETPLACE_REMOTE_CATALOG_SERVICE_GAP
- AS6_MARKETPLACE_PACKAGE_TRANSFER_SERVICE_GAP
- AS6_MARKETPLACE_SEARCH_SERVICE_GAP
- AS6_MARKETPLACE_CACHE_SERVICE_GAP
- AS6_MARKETPLACE_HEALTH_SERVICE_GAP
- AS6_MARKETPLACE_TELEMETRY_SERVICE_GAP

AEC rules:
- Marketplace Production Services must expose service registry.
- Marketplace Production Services must expose remote catalog sync facade.
- Marketplace Production Services must expose upload/download package facades.
- Marketplace Production Services must expose search, cache, health and telemetry.
