# AS6 Next Major EPIC Selection After EPIC011 Root Cause

Root cause:
After EPIC011 baseline registration, AS6 completed platform engineering and required a portfolio decision to start application engineering without mutating stable platform baselines.

Resolution:
Selected AS6_EPIC012_CRM_FOUNDATION as the next application EPIC and registered EPIC_TYPE=APPLICATION with PLATFORM_MUTATION=FALSE and IMPLEMENTATION_AUTHORIZED=FALSE.
