# AS6 Marketplace Administration AEC P33

Failure classes:
- AS6_MARKETPLACE_ADMINISTRATION_DRIFT
- AS6_MARKETPLACE_MODERATION_GAP
- AS6_MARKETPLACE_PUBLISHER_ADMIN_GAP
- AS6_MARKETPLACE_PLUGIN_BLOCKLIST_GAP
- AS6_MARKETPLACE_VERSION_REVOCATION_GAP
- AS6_MARKETPLACE_ADMIN_AUDIT_GAP

AEC rules:
- Marketplace Administration must expose moderation queue.
- Marketplace Administration must support trusted publisher approval.
- Marketplace Administration must support key registration and rotation.
- Marketplace Administration must support plugin blocking and version revocation.
- Marketplace Administration must expose admin audit log.
